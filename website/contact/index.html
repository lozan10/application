Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about SMARI Institute.

What is the cost of Level 1 courses?

Level 1 courses at SMARI are priced at UGX 395,000 for 3 months of comprehensive training. This includes course materials, instruction, and access to our facilities. We offer quality education with transparent pricing and flexible payment options.

How long do the courses take to complete?

Our Level 1 courses are intensive one-month programs designed to give you practical, job-ready skills quickly. Classes typically run 5 days a week with flexible scheduling options.

What are the admission requirements?

Level 1 courses have no specific academic requirements. We welcome applicants from all backgrounds who are motivated to learn and develop new skills. You just need to be committed to attending classes regularly.

Do you help with job placement after graduation?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated recruitment platform and career services team that helps connect our graduates with employers. Over 85% of our graduates find employment within 6 months of completion.

Can I visit the campus before applying?

Yes! We encourage prospective students to visit our campuses. Contact us to schedule a tour where you can meet faculty, see our facilities, and learn more about our programs.

What happens after Level 1?

After successfully completing Level 1, you can progress to Level 2 and Level 3 courses for more advanced training. You can also immediately start working in your field or use our job placement services.
