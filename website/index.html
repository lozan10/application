About SMARI Institute

Transforming lives through innovative education and practical skills development designed for the modern African workforce.

Our Mission

To facilitate opportunities for youths regardless of background, to discover and express their abilities and skillsets through purposefully designed courses that reflect modern educational trends and industry needs.

Our Vision

Breeding a dynamic, visionary and creative younger generation that will lead Africa into a prosperous future through innovation, entrepreneurship, and skilled professional excellence.

Our Approach

Practical experience delivered by industry players using education technology that reflects the most modern trends and research in education, ensuring our graduates are work-ready from day one.
